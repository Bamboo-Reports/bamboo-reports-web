# Marketing Material

Headline + description copy for the Bamboo Reports marketing page. Each entry is written to sell the feature to a prospective buyer, not to document how it works internally.

---

## 1. Data View

**Headline:** Every account, center, and contact in one clean, fast table.

**Description:** Stop digging through scattered spreadsheets and outdated PDFs. Bamboo Reports puts every Account, Center, Prospect, and Service into a single, structured data grid built for speed at scale. Sort, scan, and drill into any row without the lag, and open a full detail view on any record with one click. It's the difference between searching for data and simply having it in front of you.

---

## 2. Extended Data View

**Headline:** Open any account, see every center behind it.

**Description:** An account name is just the headline. Click into any account and Bamboo Reports unfolds the full picture: every delivery center tied to that company, mapped and tabulated side by side with headcount, location, and center type. See at a glance whether a company runs one office or twenty, and filter down to exactly the centers you care about, all without leaving the account you're already looking at.

---

## 3. Filters (Account, Centers, & Prospect Attributes)

**Headline:** Ask precise questions, get precise answers.

**Description:** Broad lists aren't insight, precision is. Bamboo Reports gives you a full filtering engine across Account, Center, and Prospect attributes: country, industry, revenue, headcount, technology stack, function, seniority, and more. Include or exclude any combination, slide revenue and employee ranges into exact bands, and search by name with alias-aware matching that finds a company even by its brand name or former identity. Build the exact slice of the market you're targeting in seconds.

---

## 4. Map

**Headline:** See where the opportunity actually is.

**Description:** Numbers on a spreadsheet don't show you geography, a map does. Bamboo Reports visualizes thousands of delivery centers on an interactive cluster map, and rolls that same data up into a state-level heat map for a bird's-eye view of where activity is concentrated. Zoom into a city, pan across a region, or step back and see the whole footprint at once. Geography becomes a filter, not a guess.

---

## 5. Quick Actions

**Headline:** Everything you need, one click away.

**Description:** Refresh your data, check what's changed, switch to dark mode, or manage your account, all from a single, always-accessible action bar. No hunting through menus or reloading the page to catch up on the latest updates. Quick Actions keeps the essentials right where your hand already is, so the dashboard stays out of your way and lets you focus on the data.

---

## 6. Saved Filters

**Headline:** Build it once, use it forever.

**Description:** The perfect filter combination shouldn't be a one-time effort. Save any filter configuration to the cloud and pull it back up in a click, whether that's a weekly prospecting list, a recurring board report, or a standing view of a specific market segment. Share saved filters with teammates so the whole team works from the same view, no re-explaining the criteria, no version drift. Set it up once, reuse it for as long as it's relevant.
